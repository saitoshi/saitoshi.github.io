# New Semester New Study Pattern Required #
I was always told to be aware of both ICS 311 and ICS 314, and now actually taking the two courses I see why people warned me about it. <br />
After a week of ICS 314, I learned that I have yet to make a better study schedule and that having fun has low precendence this semester. While Javascript is not the first computer programing language that I have learned, it is a little foreign compared to it. Javascript itself was similar to Python and learning was not the difficult part, but the amount of exercises were challenging. Which makes me realize that if I am struggling in the early part of the semester, I need to redo my schedule for the Spring 2020 semester. <br/> 
Because of this course not having a consistent pattern with its work days, it does make taking this course and learning Javascript stressful. For instance, ICS 311 Algorithms course has a strict week schedule that everything operates in a strict way. In ICS 314, while we do have a schedule, there is no pattern to the schedule itself. For example, there are no patterns to the due dates with the assignements. While the easiest solution to this problem would just be create a calendar, this does not easily solve the problem for a person with stress and anxiety problems. </br>
Depsite my negative thoughts towards the class, I still think this course is beneficial. While most people say, "all work is no fun," I personally feel that statement does not apply to the current era. Sooner or later, most work will be replaced by AI and robots that only way for a human being is to prove its worth and existence is through work. And with this course being demanding in the work load, it tests us on who can surivive or not.

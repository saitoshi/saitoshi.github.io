Project Title: (in progress) STEM Navigator 

Overview :
The Problem: The University of Hawaii at Manoa (UH Manoa for short) offers multiple different fields of STEM, however, there are those who are either unsure or have a major that they are interested in. As a result, many students might change their majors back and forth, therefore, extending their time at school. 

The Solution: STEM Navigator is an application for students to view what each STEM major offers along with the current or graduate student comment/suggestion for those that are coming into the major.

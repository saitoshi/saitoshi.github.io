With half of 2020 coming to an end, that also implies the 2020 spring semester coming to an end too. Initially, I did not have any strong knowledge about software engineering, but after taking the course I feel more confident. However, while the course taught us essential skills for software engineering, can they be applied for other subjects? The response to that question is yes. One of the major reasons is because Professor Phillip Johnson’s ICS 314 course is not your traditional college lecture course. Instead, it incorporates group work and communication methods that were frequent in primary school but applied for college students. The three main ideas to take back from this course was Project Management, Ethics, and Functional Programming. Despite this course centering on these topics around general software development, they can be taken outside from it.

### Project Management 
 
As the name implies, project management is the functional ability to work within teams. To conclude the semester, Professor Johnson broke us into teams of three to five people and had us use GitHub to manage within our teams. This was essential, especially during the COVID-19 lockdown.To begin with we used issue listings to create a “what to do” list. Once the list is made the members can individually select which to do and work on it. To help create a smooth work pace, each individual was created so that it can be finished within two days. While I cannot say for other teams, my team has used the communication tool, Discord to do frequent communication. As mentioned before, because of COVID-19 state lockdown, we had to adapt to communicate frequently since we cannot meet during regular class sessions.   

However, the techniques mentioned in the previous can be used for other activities. For instance, unlike my peers in my course I am not a Computer Science or Computer Engineering major. I am a Mathematics major, and despite not being the general major group to take this course I can still use the methods learned. For instance, when I take part in research we break up work so it can be done within one to three day span. And when there are issues, we try to communicate within a selected platform and make a selected time and date to meet. Overall, project management tactics were used in this course were not different. 

### Ethics 

Next is one of the hot topics that is brought between different disciplines: ethics. By definition from the Oxford dictionary, “moral principles that govern a person’s behavior or conducting activity“. Especially, with the COVID-19 outbreak going around the United States, many people’s ethics are placed to test. But is ethics found in software engineering different from other disciplines?
